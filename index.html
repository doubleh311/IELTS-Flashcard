Introduction

IELTS Flashcard is a totally free, open and flexible application that helps you study essential vocabulary for IELTS English Language Test.

The application provides you with the opportunity to study and prepare for IELTS test and focuses on building up your vocabulary bank for IELTS. The words are classified into topics from familiar to advanced ones so you will find it easy to master them.

Studying new words by flashcards has been an increasing trend in language learning. This application will offer you the chance to study in this way. Flashcards will help you study new words with their definitions, pronunciation and example sentences in the particular context. In addition, a pronunciation guide is integrated in order to help you improve your pronunciation while studying.

Come and enjoy studying with us!
